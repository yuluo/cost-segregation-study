# Membership Categories

## Overview
ASCSP membership is divided into **Technical** and **Non-technical** categories, each serving different professional roles within the cost segregation industry.

## Technical Memberships

### Member
**Entry-level technical designation**

**Requirements:**
- Minimum **1,000 hours** of direct cost segregation experience (1+ year)
- **Bachelor's degree** in an approved field (engineering, architecture, construction management, etc.)
  - **OR** 3,000 hours of experience without approved degree
- Must pass the **Member Exam**

**Qualifications:**
- Can perform cost segregation studies
- Cannot certify reports under ASCSP standards

### Certified Cost Segregation Professional (CCSP)
**Highest technical designation**

**Requirements:**
- Minimum **7,000 hours** of direct cost segregation experience (7+ years)
- **Without approved degree:** 9,000 hours over 9 years
- Must pass the **Certified Exam**

**Qualifications:**
- **Only CCSPs can certify cost segregation reports** per ASCSP standards
- Represents the highest level of expertise and experience
- Expected to maintain professional development

## Non-Technical Memberships

### Associate
**Non-technical membership category**

**Requirements:**
- No experience requirements
- No examination required

**Intended For:**
- Professionals interested in cost segregation but not conducting studies
- Support staff and administrative personnel
- Those exploring the field before pursuing technical membership

### Honorary Member
**Special recognition category**

**Selection Process:**
- Board nomination only
- Reserved for individuals who have made significant contributions to:
  - The cost segregation profession
  - The ASCSP organization
  - Industry advancement and education

**Qualifications:**
- No formal requirements
- Recognition of exceptional service or achievement
