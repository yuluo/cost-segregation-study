# Materials and Supplies (M&S)

## Overview

The TPR provides specific thresholds for small-cost items used in business operations, simplifying treatment of incidental items and components.

## Expensing Threshold

A UOP can be expensed as a material or supply if:
- Its economic useful life is 12 months or less, **OR**
- The unit cost is $200 or less

## Categories of Materials and Supplies

- Components acquired to maintain, repair, or improve a UOP
- Fuel, lubricants, water, and similar items
- Property with economic useful life of 12 months or less
- Property with acquisition or production cost of $200 or less

## Spare Parts

Special rules apply to specific types of spare parts:

### Rotable Spare Parts

Parts that are:
- Acquired for installation on a UOP
- Removable, generally repaired, and reinstalled or stored for later installation
- Temporary or standby emergency spare parts
