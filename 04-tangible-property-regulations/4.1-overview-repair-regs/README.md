# Overview of the "Repair Regs"

## Treasury Decision 9636

Final Tangible Property Regulations provide rules for applying sections 162(a) and 263(a). These regulations apply to costs paid to acquire, produce, or improve tangible property.

## Purpose

The regulations were designed to:
- Clarify and expand the standards for whether an expenditure is a deductible repair or a capital improvement
- Provide more certainty for taxpayers

## Effective Date

- Proposed versions existed earlier
- Final regulations became effective for tax years beginning on or after January 1, 2014
